About Me

Research Focus

I am a robotics engineer and researcher passionate about developing intelligent autonomous systems. My work spans optimal control theory, reinforcement learning, and bio-inspired robotics, with applications in underwater vehicles, aerial systems, and environmental monitoring.

Currently working as a Project Associate at IIT Bombay, I focus on Venus atmospheric dynamics and autonomous airship control. My research combines rigorous mathematical modeling with practical implementation to create robust, efficient robotic systems.
