About

I am a robotics engineer and researcher passionate about developing intelligent autonomous systems. Currently working as a Project Associate at IIT Bombay's Department of Aerospace Engineering, I focus on Venus atmospheric dynamics and autonomous airship control systems.

My research combines rigorous mathematical modeling with practical implementation to create robust, efficient robotic systems. I have extensive experience in reinforcement learning, optimal control, and bio-inspired robotics, with applications spanning underwater vehicles, aerial systems, and environmental monitoring.

Research Focus

Optimal control, reinforcement learning, bio-inspired robotics, and physics-informed modeling for control systems in autonomous vehicles and environmental applications.
